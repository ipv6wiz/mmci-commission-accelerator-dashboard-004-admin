Registration Form Help

Even if you entered some data on the SignUp form please enter it again here

  • Your entries must match exactly what is on file at the DRE
  • Steps must be completed in order. You can click Next or the step number to move to the next step.
  • Your entries are saved after each step. If you logout before completing the form the data you entered will be restored to the form when you next login
  • When you have filled all the required fields you can submit your form for verification & approval by clicking the Submit Form button in the Final step (Submit for Verification)
  • If you have any questions or need further help please contact our support team
